About Lazaboon

Lazaboon is a multi-category e-commerce platform designed to make everyday shopping simple, reliable, and accessible. We offer a wide range of products, including furniture, electronics, clothing, and lifestyle essentials, all in one convenient online store.

Our goal is to provide customers with quality products, transparent policies, secure payment options, and dependable delivery services across Jordan. Lazaboon focuses on customer satisfaction by offering clear ordering processes, fair return and refund policies, and responsive customer support at every step of the shopping journey.

Whether you are furnishing your home, upgrading your electronics, or refreshing your wardrobe, Lazaboon is committed to delivering value, convenience, and trust.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
1. What products can I buy on Lazaboon?

You can purchase a wide variety of products, including furniture, electronics, clothing, and other household and lifestyle items.

2. How do I place an order?

You can browse products, add items to your cart, proceed to checkout, enter shipping and payment details, and place your order. An order is confirmed only after successful payment.

3. How will I know my order is confirmed?

Once payment is successful, you will receive an order confirmation message, usually by email. Each order is assigned a unique order number.

4. How long does delivery take?

Inside Amman: 1–2 business days

Outside Amman: 2–3 business days

Delivery times may vary due to demand or courier-related issues.

5. What are the delivery charges?

Inside Amman:

3 JD for Khalda, Sweileh, and Jabal Amman

4 JD for other areas

Outside Amman: 5 JD

6. Can I track my order?

Yes. Once your order is shipped, tracking details will be provided via a tracking link or through the order history section in your account.

7. What is your return policy?

Items can be returned within 14 days of purchase if they are unused, in original packaging, and meet the return conditions. Digital products can be returned on the same day of purchase.

8. Which items are non-returnable?

Non-returnable items include sale items, stationery, and clothing intended for private or personal use.

9. When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed after the returned item is inspected. Processing may take several business days, plus additional time depending on your bank or payment provider.

10. Can I cancel my order?

Orders can usually be canceled before shipping for a full refund. If the order has already been shipped, you may need to follow the return process.

11. What payment methods are accepted?

Lazaboon accepts credit cards, debit cards, other supported online payment methods, cash on delivery, and in-store payment (availability may vary).

12. How can I get help if I have an issue?

You can contact Lazaboon customer support to track orders, check refund status, understand policies, or receive step-by-step assistance.
